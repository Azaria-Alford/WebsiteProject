I’ve been always into art and design and expressing myself‘s from a young age. I was always getting my hands on something like a pencil or a paintbrush and painting or drawing on anything I could find. In the past, I have painted on regular canvases to shoe to shoeboxes. When I was younger I remember watching ink masters with my dad. It has always been one of my favorite moment to share with my dad every time it comes on because we are both into art and different kind of art styles. To this day we still watch it together. So when I got older I really wanted to be a tattoo artist when I realize I can leave my artwork on somebody permanently. Earlier this year I decided to get serious about that idea and got my first tattoo kit in January. Since then I’ve been trying to better myself and learn all the skills possible.
